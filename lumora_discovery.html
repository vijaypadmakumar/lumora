The reality

A claim is not the same as proof.

✕
Buyers receive supplier-declared quality data every day. Most of it is unverifiable. They have learned to distrust it.
✕
Without independent testing, buyers build in a quality discount. Your supplier earns less. Your margin shrinks.
✕
You cannot show a buyer the difference between high-quality and low-quality material without third-party analysis.
✕
Quality disputes after delivery damage relationships and can end the business entirely.
© 2026 Lumora. All rights reserved.

The reality

Compliance is not fast enough.

✕
RED III requires chain-of-custody documentation from the first collection point back to the producer. Building this manually takes days per deal.
✕
Industrial buyers are required to hold this documentation. If you cannot provide it quickly, they will find someone who can.
✕
Errors in compliance documents cause delays. Delayed shipments damage relationships with buyers.
✕
The compliance burden does not get smaller as you grow. It increases with every transaction.
